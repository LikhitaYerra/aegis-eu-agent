# Entry into force and application

Article 113 states that Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 applies generally from 2 August 2026.

Chapters I and II, including the general provisions, prohibited-practices chapter, and AI literacy
obligation, apply from 2 February 2025. Chapter III Section 4, Chapter V on general-purpose AI,
Chapter VII, Chapter XII, and Article 78 apply from 2 August 2025, except Article 101. Article 6(1)
and its corresponding obligations apply from 2 August 2027.

The Regulation is binding in its entirety and directly applicable in all Member States. Specific
transitional provisions and later legislation or guidance must be checked when assessing the
status of an obligation on a particular date.
